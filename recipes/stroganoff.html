Stroganoff

Let’s do a straightforward, tasty beef stroganoff recipe that hits all the flavor notes without being fussy.

Tasty Beef Stroganoff Recipe (Serves 4)

Ingredients

  • 500g (1 lb) beef sirloin or tenderloin, thinly sliced into strips
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200g (7 oz) mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter or oil
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream or sour cream
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste (optional, adds richness)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or egg noodles, for serving

Instructions

  1. Slice beef into thin strips and season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Sear beef quickly until browned but not fully cooked, then remove from skillet and set aside.
  4. Sauté onion and garlic until soft and fragrant.
  5. Add mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and start to brown.
  6. Stir in tomato paste and Dijon mustard, mixing well.
  7. Return beef to the pan and pour in heavy cream or sour cream.
  8. Simmer gently 5–7 minutes until sauce thickens and beef is cooked through. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve over rice or egg noodles.

Now just put in a plate and eat it!
